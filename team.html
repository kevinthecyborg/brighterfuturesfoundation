Team

The Brighter Futures Foundation Team

Our dedicated team at the Brighter Futures Foundation is committed to empowering students through scholarships, mental health support, and personal development programs. With diverse backgrounds and a shared passion for fostering resilience and creating opportunities, we work tirelessly to equip young people with the tools they need to build brighter futures for themselves and their communities.

President & Rising Star Treasurer Board Member

Forrest Fletcher

We are thrilled to introduce Forrest Fletcher, the President of the Brighter Futures Foundation. Forrest's dedication and leadership are instrumental in driving our mission forward. As a 2024 Rising Star award recipient, he embodies the spirit of perseverance and excellence that our foundation stands for.

Executive Director

Amber Fletcher

We are thrilled to introduce Amber Fletcher as the Executive Director of the Brighter Futures Foundation! Amber brings a wealth of experience and passion to our foundation. Her dedication to promoting education and mental health aligns perfectly with our mission. We are confident that her leadership will help us reach new heights and make a significant impact in our community.

Secretary Board Member

Steven Garcia

We are thrilled to announce that Steven Garcia, Marketing Director at Allways Atascosa Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram, has been elected to our Board of Directors. Steven brings a wealth of experience in marketing and strategic planning, and we are excited to have his expertise and passion for community development on our team.

Board Member

Clint Brandenberger

We are excited to introduce Clint Brandenberger as the newest board member of the Brighter Futures Foundation! Clint is the owner of The American Hail Company, bringing a wealth of experience and dedication to our foundation. His commitment to excellence and community service aligns perfectly with our mission to support education and mental health initiatives.
